What is Debreate ?

The goal behind Debreate is to make developing for Debian based Linux distributions more appealing, with an easy to use interface for packaging applications, artwork, media, themes, etc.

FAQ

Can I use Debreate for free ?
Of course! Debreate is free and under MIT licence.
How can I contribute ?
Using Debreate and report bugs is a good start to contribute. You can also translate Debreate in your favorite language. Finally, if you are a developer, fork Debreate and propose changes!
